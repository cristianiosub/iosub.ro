About me

Where product leadership meets cybersecurity

Product management and cybersecurity don't usually sit in the same room. Over nearly two decades, I've built a career precisely in that gap — designing digital products on one side, testing their resilience on the other.

At White Hat Technology, I lead cybersecurity audits, penetration tests, and physical security assessments for public institutions and private companies across Romania. Alongside that, I develop AI voice agent solutions — conversational systems deployed in public sector contexts, built to Romanian-language and GDPR standards.

I founded and lead CyberShield, Romania's non-profit association for cybersecurity education, and coordinate CyberSkill.ro, a national training platform. I participate in Locked Shields (NATO CCDCOE) and CyDEx (organized by SRI), evaluate online store security annually as part of the GPeC jury, and hold active OWASP membership.

Outside of tech, I'm a certified GLOCK Club Firearms Instructor.
